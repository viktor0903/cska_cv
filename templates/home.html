Introduction of the team

CSKA Sofia, established in 1948, is a renowned football club in Bulgaria with a history filled with triumphs. The team has secured numerous domestic championships, international recognition, and an impressive tally of 31 trophies in the national championship.

The club's home matches are played at the Vasil Levski National Stadium, where passionate fans come together to support their beloved team. The vibrant atmosphere at the stadium is something truly remarkable.

CSKA Sofia has a strong tradition of developing young talents, and over the years, it has produced some of the best footballers in the country. The dedication and hard work of the players, along with the coaching staff, have made CSKA Sofia a force to be reckoned with in Bulgarian football.

The club continues to strive for excellence, both on and off the field. With a commitment to community involvement and youth development, CSKA Sofia is not just a football club but a symbol of pride for the city and its supporters.
